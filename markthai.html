About Mark Thai Food Box Website

Mark Thai Food Box is a Thai restaurant located in Seattle, WA. As part of my freelance work, my client (Mark Thai Food Box) wanted their own e-commerce website to advertise their business and sell their products online.

My Responsibilities

As the web designer and developer of the project, I designed and developed the website using HTML/CSS, Javscript, and Node.js.

The website has full eCommerce features, such as shopping cart, checkout, and payment.
